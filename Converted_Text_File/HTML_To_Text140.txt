It is a professional certification given by Institution of Engineers India Limited (IEIL). This is an important degree for students who are unable to pursue regular engineering degree from a Technical University or Engineering College in India. This certification is considered as the equivalent to the degree of engineering including the major streams of engineering like Electronics, Mechanical and Computer Science. Once a student completes this certification, he can opt for the Masters Degree from any recognized university. He can also work in various government or private sector companies in his field of specialization.There are two types of membership in this certification:1) Technician Member: Its minimum criteria is to pass at least 12th standard with 45% minimum aggregate.2) Senior Technician Member: There are two mandatory qualifications held for becoming a senior technician member.Note: The candidate's age must be at least 18 years and candidate should have diploma or degree in technology or engineering valid by the council.ExamsThe exams of AMIE are held two times in a year in summer and winter or June and December respectively. The date and details of the exam are released in the Technical Journal. The IEIL has divided the exam into two sections; Section A and Section B.Section A is mandatory for all the candidates. In Section B, the candidate is supposed to choose a specific branch of engineering program. After passing the section A, the candidate can appear for the Section B and if he clears at least 5 papers of Section B, he becomes eligible for the Laboratory experiments. The project work comprises 10 papers; six compulsory and three optional.UsageAMIE is totally equivalent to the graduation degree in engineering, even a certified student also can write for the examinations like:Civil servicesIndian engineering services (IES)Graduate Record Examinations (GRE)Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE)Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT)Common Admission Test (CAT)Next TopicFull Form? prev next ?Help Others, Please ShareLearn Latest TutorialsOpenpyxlTallyGodotSpring BootGradleUMLANNES6FlutterSelenium PyFirebaseCobolPreparationAptitudeReasoningVerbal A.InterviewCompanyTrending TechnologiesAIAWSSeleniumCloudHadoopReactJSD.
