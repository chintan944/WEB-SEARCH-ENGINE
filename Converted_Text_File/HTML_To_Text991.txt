It is an undergraduate medical degree in Homeopathy; an alternative approach to medicine. The duration of the course is around five year. The course is followed by an internship which starts after the students complete the final examination.Homeopathy emerged in the year 1796 and its founder is Samuel Hahnemann. It is based on the principle that the substances responsible for the symptoms of a disease in healthy people can be used to treat similar symptoms in ailing people.Eligibility for BHMSA student who has passed 12th class with physics, chemistry and Biology as the main subjects is eligible to enroll in BHMS. The admission is based on the merit. There are also various joint entrance exams for M.B.B.S, B.D.S and B.H.M.S courses. The subjects included in this course are Homeopathic Pharmacy, Homeopathic Material Medica, Homeopathic Philosophy, Bio-Chemistry, Homoeopathic Repertory, Practice of Medicine, etc.Specialization in BHMSAfter successfully completing the course, one can pursue specialization in Homeopathy. There are various options for specialization in Homeopathy like Pediatrics, Infertility, Psychiatry, Skin Specialist, etc.Some Popular BHMS CollegesNehru Homoeopathic Medical College, New DelhiSmt.
