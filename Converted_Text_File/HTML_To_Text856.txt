It refers to the Department of AYUSH. Earlier it was known as the Department of Indian System of Medicine and Homeopathy (ISM&H). It was renamed Department of AYUSH in November 2003 with a view to give more emphasis on the development of Education and Research in Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy. In 2003, it was operating under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. Today, it is under the Ministry of AYUSH which was established on 9 November 2014. It is headquartered in New Delhi.
