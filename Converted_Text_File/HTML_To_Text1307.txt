It is a critical analysis done by a credit analyst or a banker to check the credibility of a company which has applied for a loan. It involves the in-depth analysis of current and projected financial statements of a loan applicant. This analysis shows the credit worthiness of a company which reflects the ability of a company to repay the loan.
