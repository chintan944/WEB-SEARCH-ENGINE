It is a regional development bank that is Asian in character. It was established to reduce poverty and foster economic growth and cooperation in Asia and the Pacific. It assists in the socio-economic development in member countries by providing loans, grants and technical assistance. It has 67 members, 48 of which are from Asia and Pacific region. It is headquartered at Mandaluyong, Philippines and as of July 2017, Takehiko Nakao is the president of ADB.Areas of FocusADB operations give emphasis on the following areas:InfrastructureEnvironmentEducationRegional integrationFinance Sector developmentBrief HistoryThe idea of Asian Development Bank conceived in the early 1960s. Later, a resolution was passed at the first Ministerial Conference on Asian Economic Cooperation in 1963 to establish the ADB.On 19 December 1966, ADB was established with 31 members and Takeshi Watanabe as its first President.
