release of an Android operating system, and it is the 17th version. Android 10 has developed under the codename "Android Q". Google initially announced it on March 13, 2019. The first beta version of Android 10 was released on the same day. Its second beta version was released on April 3, 2019.On September 3, 2019, the stable version of Android 10 was released. It contains features like new permissions to access location in the background, floating setting panel, support for an AV1 video codec, support for biometric authentication, WPA3 Wi-Fi security.Features of Android 10There are various new features added to the Android 10 version. Some features are as follows:An improved file appWi-Fi sharing via QR codePrivacy and securityPlatformLive captionSmart replySound AmplifierGesture NavigationDark themeLocation controlsSecurity updatesFocus ModeFamily LinkAn improved files appGoogle's previous Files app does not contain an on-fillers affair, and it has not a shortcut in the app drawer. However, Android 10 has an improved Files app on a shortcut; it also offers a universal search bar at the top, a reversed UI, and quick access to other apps.Wi-Fi sharing via QR codeGoogle adopts a feature to share Wi-Fi credentials via QR code. This functionality has been seen in Huawei and Xiaomi smartphones for a long time. This feature is easy to use; you can tap on the Wi-Fi connection and click on the share button. After that, authenticate Wi-Fi with your phone's password or a fingerprint. You will see a QR code, and your friend's device needs to scan this QR code to achieve access.Privacy and securityThere are lots of significant privacy, and security changes introduced in Android 10. A user is able to restrict an app to access the location when they are active in the foreground. The storage access permission is also changed in Android 10, which is known as "Scoped storage". This permission allows accessing the file of external storage only.PlatformAndroid 10 supports a WPA3 encryption protocol technique. This encryption technique provides strong encryption for Wi-Fi. At the same time, Android 10 also supports Dual-SIM dual-standby (DSDS). The first DSDS was initially available on Pixel 3a and Pixel 3a XL.Live captionWith one tap, live caption automatically adds subtitle to podcasts, audio messages, and videos, even things you record yourself. The real-time captions are created using on-device machine learning, and it will arrive when the speech is detected.Smart replyIn Android 10, you get suggested response and sometimes even more to your message. You may also receive a suggested action for it. For example, if your friend asks for a ride, your phone will recommend you text "". It also displays correct directions in Google Maps.Sound AmplifierUsing sound amplifier, Android 10 phones filters the background noise, fine-tune to how you hear best. You can listen to more clear to your friends, listening to a lecture, watching TV by plug in your headphones.Gesture NavigationAndroid 10 has gesture navigation, which is more wise and faster than ever. You can move forward and backward, swipe up to view your open apps, and pull up the home screen. All these activities can be performed with super smooth.Dark themeThe dark theme feature was being requested for a long time. Finally, the dark theme is added to Android 10. This functionality will become active when you enable the battery saver option, or it can be activated through a quick-tile setting. The dark mode also affects the photos, Google podcasts, and search. The dark mode is certainly one of the most famous Android 10 device's features.Privacy controlsUsing Android 10, you can able to control your privacy. That is because you get a new, smarter control using which you can decide when and how data of your device is shared. So, the device does the task, and you will be having rest.Security updatesAn Android phone already receives regular security updates. However, in Android 10, you will receive them even easier and faster with the help of the Google Play system. The updates, privacy fixes, and important security can be directly sent to your phone from Google Play. In the same way, your other apps also get an update.Focus ModeFocus Mode is one of the outstanding features that users are facing in Android 10. It is an extension of the Digital Wellbeing suite. As the name indicates, this mode helps you to eject distracting applications by focusing and holding their notifications.Family LinkYou can even guide your children as they play, learn, and explore online. You have to set view app activity, screen time limits, content restrictions, and manage apps.
