It is developed by Google for soundbars, set-top boxes, digital media players, and TVs with native applications. It is a replacement for Google TV. The Android TV platform was first launched in June 2014.This platform has also been adopted as smart TV middleware by a company such as Sharp and Sony.HistoryAndroid TV was first announced at Google I/O in June 2014, as the successor to the unsuccessful Google TV.Google discloses the first Android TV device, the name Nexus Player developed by Asus at a hardware event in October 2014.An ADT-2 was released earlier than the release of Android TV 9.0.The Android TV and ADT-3 are both released together on December 10, 2019.FeaturesAndroid TV platform is the adaptation of the Android operating system for a set-top box. It has integrated software on smart TV hardware. The Android TV facilitates the voice input for commands and universal search across multiple services. Android TV also supports software from "Google Play Store", which includes media apps and games (however, not all the Google Play apps are compatible with Android TV).Some of the major features of Android TV are given below:Voice search via remoteNative Google Cast supportCurated content on the top rowUniversal search using top appsTons and games via Google Play StoreGoogle Assistant integrationActor and actress biosGoogle Assistant built-in Android TVIn Android TV, a Google Assistant is also integrated with visual responses. We can just say "Ok Google" or press the mic button to instantly find the latest blockbuster, dim the light, or check the score of the big game all these are performed without leaving what you are watching.The Android TV platform is also in set-top boxes and built into full-size TVs. The popular set-top boxes in this platform include the Xiaomi Mi Box s, Nvidia Shield TV.Chromecast built-in Android TVUsing Android TV, we can instantly cast our music, videos, photos, and other content that we love from our favorite device straight to our TV.The product that uses Android TVNvidia Shield (2017)The Nvidia Shield is the best box for the Android TV owners. It plays a whole host of games that we don't find on any other streaming device.
