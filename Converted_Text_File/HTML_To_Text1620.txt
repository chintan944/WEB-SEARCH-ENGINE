It is also known as SSC CPO, as it is an exam conducted by the Staff Selection Commission for the recruitment of candidates for the post of Sub-inspector (SI) of Police. It is conducted at National Level to recruit SI for various organizations like ITBI, CRPF, BSI, Delhi Police, and more.Exam Pattern: The SSC PCO 2019 Exam is conducted in different stages which are as follows:Paper I: It is a written exam of 200 marks with a duration of 2 hours. The subjects covered in this paper include General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Knowledge and General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, and English Comprehension.Paper-II: It is also a two-hour written exam of 200 marks. The subjects included in this exam are English Language and Comprehension.Physical Endurance Test: After passing the written exam, the candidates are qualified for a physical endurance test. Its difficulty level is different for male and female candidates as per their physical abilities:The vacancies for SSC CPO 2019 are expected to be:SI (Male): 97SI (Female): 53SI (CAPF): 1073SSC CPO Eligibility Criteria:Nationality: The candidate must be a citizen of India.Academic Qualification: The candidate must be a graduate in any stream from a recognized University in India.Age Limit: The minimum age required is 20 years, and the maximum age limit is 25 years.
