National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology, which operates within the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Govt. of India, has introduced the CCC course for the common people. This course aims to provide basic computer knowledge to make people computer literate. After completing this course, one can easily use computers for various purposes such as to type letters, send mails, read news, online shopping, social media and more.The duration of CCC course is 80 hours and is conducted every month of the year. There is no age limit for this course. However, the candidates are required to fill an online form available on the NIELIT official website to enroll themselves for the CCC. The examination fee is around RS. 500 plus tax.The information should be correct as it can't be edited or corrected afterwards. The fee is also not refundable under any circumstances. The minimum eligibility criterion for this course is 12th class pass candidate from any recognized Board in India, however there are few exceptions.The 80 hour duration of the course is divided into three parts:Theory takes 25 hoursTutorials take 5 hoursPracticals take 50 hoursCCC is sponsored by (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) (DOEACC society), an autonomous society, under the control of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MOE & IT).The candidates can appear in the CCC exam by three different modes, each mode has different eligibility criteria which are as follows:The candidates who are sponsored by NIELIT approved institutes that are approved to conduct CCC course, can appear in this exam irrespective of any education qualifications.The candidates, who are sponsored by government affiliate schools or colleges with a unique identity number from NIELIT for conducting CCC, can appear in this exam irrespective of any educational qualification.Direct applicants (who are not following the first two criteria) are also eligible for CCC exam irrespective of any educational qualification.Next TopicFull Forms List? prev next ?Help Others, Please ShareLearn Latest TutorialsOpenpyxlTallyGodotSpring BootGradleUMLANNES6FlutterSelenium PyFirebaseCobolPreparationAptitudeReasoningVerbal A.InterviewCompanyTrending TechnologiesAIAWSSeleniumCloudHadoopReactJSD.
