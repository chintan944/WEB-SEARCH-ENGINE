It is an undergraduate professional degree for the students who want to make a career in teaching. The degree is formerly known as Bachelor of Teaching (BT). The duration of the course is two years. After successfully completing the course, the students become eligible to teach in intermediate and high schools. In India, it is a major prerequisite for any graduate to teach at the high school level.Eligibility for B.EDYou have to clear the B.ED entrance exam to study the B.ED course. The students who have completed their undergraduate degree are eligible for the B.ED exams. For example, the students who possess Bachelor of Commerce (B. Com), Bachelor of Science (B. Sc) or Bachelor of Arts (B.A) degree are eligible for B.ED entrance exams conducted by different universities and colleges in India. The students also required to have a minimum of 50 % marks in graduation.It is a skill-based course that focuses on providing all the necessary teaching skills to the students so that they can understand different aspects of classroom teaching. The Arts students are trained to teach history, civics, geography, etc. The science graduates are trained to teach biology, physics and chemistry. The commerce graduates are trained to teach accounting, economics, etc. The course is mainly designed to impart the teaching skills and make the students familiar with different aspects of classroom teaching.After completing the B.ED, the students become eligible for M.ED (Master of Education). The National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) is the statutory body which monitors the educations courses offered by different teacher training institutes in India.Popular B.ED Entrance Exams in IndiaIGNOU B.ED Entrance ExamGuru Nanak Dev University B.ED Entrance ExamMaharashtra B.ED Common Entrance TestLucknow University B.ED Entrance ExamTelangana State Education Common Entrance TestUttar Pradesh B.ED Joint Entrance ExamPunjab University B.ED Application FormJammu Kashmir B.ED Entrance ExamHimachal Pradesh University B.ED Entrance TestGujarat University B.ED Entrance TestPopular B.ED Colleges in IndiaLady Irwin College, DelhiBombay Teacher's Training College, MumbaiKasturi Ram College of Higher Education, DelhiAndhra University, VisakhapatanamGovernment College of Education, ChandigarhD.M.
